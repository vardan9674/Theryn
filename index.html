Theryn — Free Coaching & Gym App

Theryn is a free coaching and workout-tracking app for personal trainers, gym coaches, and athletes. Build routines, track workouts, message your coach in real time, and log body metrics — all in one dashboard.

What you get

  • Routine builder — design workouts once, assign to any athlete
  • Workout tracking — sets, reps, weights, rest, personal records
  • Real-time messaging — chat with your coach or athlete with read receipts and typing indicators
  • Body metrics — weight, measurements, progress over time
  • Coach dashboard — manage many athletes, see who trained today, track payments

Who it's for

Personal trainers running an online coaching practice, strength coaches who work with multiple athletes, and anyone who wants a cleaner alternative to spreadsheets and screenshots for programming.

Pricing

Free for coaches and athletes.
